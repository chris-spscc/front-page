For the third time in a row, SPSCC named one of nation's Top 150 community colleges

SPSCC has been recognized by the Aspen Institute College Excellence Program and will apply to compete for the 2021 Aspen Prize for Community College Excellence and $1 million dollars in prize funds. The College Excellence Program selects its top 150 every two years from a pool of nearly 1,000 public two-year colleges nationwide. SPSCC was also recognized as a top 150 community college in Aspen Institute’s 2017 and 2019 nominations.

SPSCC is joining in downtown for the holidays and OlyTwinklefest

SPSCC is getting jolly this holiday season with a cookie decorating event and a festive window display. The fun begins Sun., Dec. 1, when the public is invited to join baking & pastry arts professor Melanie Shelton to decorate gingerbread cookies and warm up after the Downtown for the Holidays kickoff parade. Don't miss our beautiful window display and a chance to vote for us in the 7th annual PBIA Oly Twinklefest! Voting is open from Nov. 30 - Jan. 1.

3D art exhibit examining themes around "walls" comes to The Gallery

Beginning Nov. 11, SPSCC will welcome Eun-Kyung Suh, a Full Professor of 3D Art at the University of Minnesota, Duluth. Suh's new work "Stay" explores the historical and modern meanings of walls through her silk 3D art. The exhibition will run Nov. 11 - Dec. 13, with an opening reception and artist talk Friday, Nov. 15, from 4:30 p.m. to 6:30 p.m.
